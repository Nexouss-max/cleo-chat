Our Features

CLEO is packed with powerful features to enhance your skincare journey:

  • Personalized Chat: Have a conversation with our AI to discuss your skin concerns and get tailored advice.
  • Ingredient Analyzer: Paste a product's ingredient list and get a detailed, easy-to-understand breakdown of what's inside.
  • Chat History: All your consultations are saved, so you can revisit past recommendations at any time.
  • Configurable AI: Choose from different AI models and customize the system prompt to adjust CLEO's personality.
  • File Uploads: Upload images of your skin or products for more accurate analysis and recommendations.
  • Voice-to-Text: Use your microphone to speak your questions directly to CLEO.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to CLEO's responses read aloud.
  • Customizable Themes: Switch between Light, Dark, Mint, and Sky themes to personalize your experience.
  • Export Chat: Save your important consultations as a text file for offline access.
